The Infinite Nexus Sabotage (Sabacc Edition)

The Mission Briefing

You are **Juno Eclipse**, the galaxy's most chaotic and effective temporal infiltrator. The **Nexus AI** is attempting to stabilize the multiverse. Your job: **Inject enough high-tier absurdity** to fracture its control.

***NEW: The Sabacc Challenge***

Before playing your main card, you can attempt the **Sabacc Challenge**. Get a hand as close to **23 or -23** as possible. A successful Sabacc hand grants a huge **Absurdity Multiplier** for the turn. A failure (a **Bomb**, over 30 or under -30) results in immediate penalties! The deck is volatile—cards can shift!

Instructions:

  • **Objective:** Survive as many chapters as possible, achieving the highest **Rebel Standing**.
  • **Sabacc Phase:** Optional pre-turn risk. Win a multiplier, or lose health.
  • **Main Phase:** Select the right White Card(s) to maximize your **Absurdity** and score.
  • **Synergy:** Match your card's theme to the Black Card's theme for a **Standing Bonus**.
  • **Game Over:** You lose if your **Hull Integrity** or **Temporal Stability** hits 0%, or if **Rebel Standing** falls below 10.
